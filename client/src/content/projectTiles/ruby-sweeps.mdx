Ruby Sweeps is a Social Gaming Platform made for US customers by Ruby Play Network.
It is a <u>commercial level</u> application developed to collaborate with in-person bingo halls.
It supports accepting Donations through Payment Providers, faciliates online social gaming, provides rewards and jackpots.
